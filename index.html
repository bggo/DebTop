DebTop

An alternative to a better WebTop

The DebTop project is an alternative for improving your WebTop experience.

The concept of DebTop is to create a Debian Wheezy ARM image inside the SDCard so you can acess it contents using chroot.

As the DepTop is an initial project, DO IT AT YOUR ON RISK, but, it`s working for us.

Dependencies

  • A least 2Gb SDCard;
  • Rooted Android with WebTop;

Tested Phones

  • Motorola Atrix
